Greninja

Gen 6 (Kalos)

Greninja was introduced in Gen 6 or Kalos along with the other starters Fennekin and Chespin as starter pokemon.

Not to mention it's hidden ability Protean which makes every move stab moves like U-turn, Gunk shot, and Ice beam even more potent.Greninja is a very fast pokemon with a base speed of 122.
